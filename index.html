Bio

Captain america real name is steven grant rogers. Steven Grant Rogers was born in Brooklyn, New York on July 4th, 1918 to Sarah and Joseph Rogers..... His father was an Army soldier who fought and died in the First World War. His mother, a nurse, died of tuberculosis........ Years later during an art class, Steve and Bucky find out that America has joined the Second World War....Captain America is the alter ego of Steve Rogers, a frail young artist enhanced to the peak of human perfection by an experimental "super-soldier serum" after joining the military to aid the United States government's efforts in World War II.
